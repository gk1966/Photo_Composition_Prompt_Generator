🎨 Photo Composition Prompt Generator

Create Prompts for Photo Composition and Editing

💡 How it works: Add 2–3 images (JPG or PNG format) that you already have and want to upload to the AI. Define which image will serve as the background and which will be added on top (as an overlay). 🚨 IMPORTANT: The generated prompts instruct the AI to use your ACTUAL uploaded images as-is without regenerating them. This ensures a non-destructive composition, similar to Photoshop layers. For each overlay image, you can choose whether to perform cutout first (background removal). Then, adjust the position, size, and other edits to create the desired composition.
