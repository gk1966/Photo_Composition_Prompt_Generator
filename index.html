2. Photo Composition Prompt Generator

This tool focuses on teaching **composition** and **visual vocabulary**. It assists users in structuring complex prompts for AI models by decomposing multi-layered image assembly into discrete operations (e.g., subject isolation, spatial positioning, scaling, blending modes). The goal is to enhance the quality and precision of generated images through the **explicit specification** of visual composition.
